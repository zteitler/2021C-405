## Course Information

**Course Number**:
: Math 405 and 505

**Course Title**:
: Abstract Algebra

**Course Description**:
: Topics in group theory, ring theory and field theory
  with emphasis on finite and solvable groups, polynomials and factorization,
  extensions of fields.


**Prerequisites**:
: MATH 301 and MATH 305
